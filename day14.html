Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my GB Wears order?

You can track your order by visiting our Order Tracking page and entering your order number (e.g., #GB-2026-001). You'll see real-time updates from processing to delivery.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging. Simply initiate a return from your Order History.

How do I find my correct size?

Check our detailed Size Guide with measurements in cm and inches. Our boxer briefs run true to size — if you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for comfort.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, Verve, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely. Cash on delivery is available for Lagos orders only.

How long does shipping take?

Lagos: 1-2 business days. Other states in Nigeria: 3-5 business days. International: 7-14 business days via DHL Express.

Our support team is available Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm WAT.
